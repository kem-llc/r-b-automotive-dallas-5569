Tire Services & Alignment

Overview

Proper tire care is essential for safety, fuel efficiency, and ride comfort. We offer tire mounting, balancing, rotation, and precision alignment using state-of-the-art equipment. Whether you need new tires or just a alignment check, our team ensures your tires wear evenly and your vehicle tracks straight.

What's included

  • Longer tire life and better fuel economy
  • Smoother ride and improved handling
  • Prevents uneven wear and premature replacement
  • We can source tires for any make and model

How it works

1

Tire Inspection

We check tread depth, tire pressure, and overall condition for damage or wear patterns.

2

Mount & Balance

We mount new tires on your rims and balance them to eliminate vibrations.

3

Alignment Check

We measure camber, caster, and toe angles and adjust them to factory specifications.

Common questions

How often should I rotate my tires?

Tire rotation is recommended every 5,000 to 7,500 miles to ensure even wear. We can do it during your oil change.

Do I need an alignment if I’m not pulling to one side?

Even if your car drives straight, alignment can be off and cause uneven wear. We recommend checking alignment annually or when installing new tires.

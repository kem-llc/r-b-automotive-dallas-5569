Common questions

How often should I replace brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 50,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits. We inspect them at every visit.

What are signs of brake problems?

Squealing, grinding, vibration, or a soft pedal indicate it's time for a brake inspection.

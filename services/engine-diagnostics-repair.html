Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car isn’t performing as it should, trust R & B Automotive to find the root cause. We use advanced diagnostic scanners and years of experience to identify issues quickly. From sensor replacements to major engine overhauls, we deliver reliable repairs that restore your vehicle’s performance.

How it works

Code Reading & Analysis

Common questions

What does the check engine light mean?

It can indicate anything from a loose gas cap to a serious engine problem. We’ll diagnose the specific code and recommend the appropriate repair.

Can you diagnose intermittent problems?

Yes, we can replicate driving conditions and use advanced tools to capture intermittent issues. Our experience helps us track down tricky problems.

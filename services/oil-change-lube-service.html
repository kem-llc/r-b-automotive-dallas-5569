Oil Change & Lube Service

Keep your engine healthy with regular oil changes using premium oils and filters.

Schedule this service

Overview

Regular oil changes are the single most important maintenance item for your vehicle. At R & B Automotive, we use high-quality synthetic and conventional oils and premium filters to protect your engine. Our quick lube service gets you in and out fast without sacrificing thoroughness.

What's included

  • Extends engine life and improves performance
  • Includes multi-point inspection for added peace of mind
  • Uses OEM-recommended oil grades and filters
  • Environmentally responsible disposal of used oil

How it works

1

Initial Check

We verify your vehicle’s make, model, and mileage to recommend the right oil type and filter.

2

Drain & Fill

We drain old oil, replace the filter, and refill with fresh oil to manufacturer specifications.

3

Final Inspection

We check fluid levels, tire pressure, and belts, then provide a report of your vehicle’s condition.

Common questions

How often should I get an oil change?

Most modern vehicles need an oil change every 5,000 to 7,500 miles. Check your owner’s manual or ask our technicians for a personalized schedule.

Do you use synthetic oil?

Yes, we offer both conventional and synthetic oil changes. We’ll recommend the best option for your car and driving habits.

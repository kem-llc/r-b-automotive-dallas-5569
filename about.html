Our story

Your Trusted Auto Mechanic in Dallas Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair with transparent pricing and exceptional customer service.

R & B Automotive was founded in 1995 by Robert and Brenda, a husband-and-wife team with a passion for cars and community. Starting as a small two-bay shop on Corinth Street, they built a reputation for integrity and skilled workmanship. Over the years, the shop grew as word spread about their fair pricing and thorough repairs.

Today, R & B Automotive continues that legacy under the same family ownership. Our team of ASE-certified technicians serves Dallas drivers with the same commitment to quality and honesty that started it all. We treat every car as if it were our own and every customer as a neighbor.
